Brownie Recipe

This brownie recipe is a classic found in many homes around the world, the texture is loved by many, as well as its chocolatey taste. Some say that it reminds them of the fond times at Grandmother's house, others just feel comforted by the warmth it leaves them with. No matter what it reminds you of, you just know you like it. That's why we have compiled a nice list of recipes for you here.


Ingredients

  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 1 and 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 cup melted butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt

  • Optional topping: 1/2 cup walnut halves


Steps

  1. Preheat ooven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C), grease 9x13 inch pan
  2. Mix sugar, flour, melted butter, eggs, cocoa powder, vanilla extract, baking powder, and salt in large bowl until combined. Spread the batter into prepared pan. Sprinkle with walnut halves.
  3. Bake in preheated oven until top is dry and edges have started to pull away from sides of the pan, 20-30 minutes. Cool before serving.
